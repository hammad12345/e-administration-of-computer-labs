Welcome to e-Administration of computer labs

Provide the solution for an organization which involve computing facilities such as Computers, Printers, LCDs, Digital Boards, ACs etc. on a 24×7 bases to carryout academic experiments for all the branches with all the equipments in good working conditions and also with all the necessary software’s installed and configured

 

Advanced functions

  • Safety
  • Security 
  • Provide schedule/time-table

Objective

  1. Keep track of the detailed information of the labs
  2. Assists in the smooth interaction between different users
  3. maintenance of available Resources
  4. Helps the HOD’s of various departments to send an extra lab request to administrator
  5. Automate the task of assigning complaints to specific technical staff based on the nature of problem
  6. Helps Technical staff/instructor to lodge complaints
